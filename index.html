ABOUT US

Café Cup is the hangout spot that you didn't know you needed until now. We believe that getting a cup of coffee should be like going to your favorite restaurant and getting your favorite meal. Every time you get that special meal, you want it to taste just as good as when you first discovered it. This is what we provide for you. A nice way to start your morning, energize your day or end your evening.
